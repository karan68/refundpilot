# RefundPilot — 3-Minute Demo Script

**Total: 2 minutes 50 seconds. Practice until it's under 3:00.**

---

## INTRO (0:00 – 0:20) — The Problem

> "Every Indian e-commerce merchant bleeds money in two ways.
>
> First — a loyal customer buys a ₹800 kurta. It arrives damaged. She waits 15 days for a refund. By day 3, she's left a 1-star review. She never comes back.
>
> Second — a serial abuser returns 8 of 12 orders. Always 'damaged in transit.' He costs the merchant ₹47,000 a year. Nobody notices.
>
> Same system. Same 15-day wait. The honest customer and the fraudster — treated identically.
>
> We built RefundPilot to fix this."

---

## WHAT IT IS (0:20 – 0:40) — Architecture in 20 Seconds

> "RefundPilot is an autonomous AI agent. Here's how it works.
>
> A refund request comes in. The agent collects 10 signals — customer refund rate, delivery contradiction, RFM analysis, cross-merchant fraud from the Pine Labs network, and more.
>
> These feed into a deterministic weighted formula. Not an LLM rolling dice — actual math. Same input, same output, every time. Auditable for RBI compliance.
>
> The score goes through a 3-tier decision engine. Score under 30 — math auto-approves. Over 70 — math auto-escalates. The 31-to-70 gray zone? That's where GLM-5 enters a ReAct reasoning loop — thinking, acting, observing — true agentic AI.
>
> Actions fire through Pine Labs — real orders, real payment links, real refund API calls. Vision analysis runs through Groq's Llama-4 for evidence photo verification. Backend is Python FastAPI, frontend is React, database is SQLite with 9 seeded customer profiles."

**[Show: Architecture diagram — point to: 10 signals → weighted score → 3-tier → Pine Labs actions]**
